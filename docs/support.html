How do I connect Apple Health?

On first launch, JENNA asks for permission to read your cycling and running workouts from Apple Health. Tap "Allow." If you skipped it, go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > JENNA and enable access. JENNA only reads your workouts — it never writes anything back to Health.

Why aren't my workouts showing up?

Make sure (1) Health access is enabled for JENNA in iOS Settings, (2) the workouts are recorded as cycling or running in Apple Health, and (3) you're connected to the internet so data can sync. Pulling to refresh in the app usually does the trick.

How do I switch between cycling and running?

You can switch your active sport anytime in the app. Each sport keeps its own separate history, stats, streaks, and personal records, so nothing gets mixed up.

How does XP and leveling work?

Every workout earns XP that moves you through 30 levels. As you progress, you unlock collectible level badges (between levels 2 and 15), achievement badges (12 per sport), and upgrades for your virtual garage.

How do streaks work?

Streaks reward consistency day to day. Keep logging workouts to keep your streak alive — enable push notifications and JENNA will remind you before it's at risk.

Is my data private and backed up?

Yes. Your data is stored on your device and securely synced to the cloud under your account, so it's backed up and available across your devices. We don't collect location, heart rate, or device identifiers, and we never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does JENNA cost anything?

No. JENNA has no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated data. You can also request deletion by emailing us.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the sign-in screen to receive a reset link at your registered email.
