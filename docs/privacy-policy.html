Privacy Policy

JENNA — Run & Bike Tracker · Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how JENNA ("JENNA", the "App", "we", "us", or "our"), operated by Blake McLean, collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using JENNA, you agree to the practices described here. This policy is designed to comply with the Apple App Store Review Guidelines, Apple HealthKit requirements, and Google Play Developer Program Policies.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect only what is needed to run the App:

  • Account information: your email address and a password, used to create and secure your account. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and stored in hashed, non-reversible form — we never see your plain-text password.
  • App preferences: a small set of settings such as your active sport, indoor cycling speed, and notification preferences.
  • Workout data from Apple Health (with your permission): read-only workout summaries including distance, duration, calories, and activity type for your cycling and running workouts. We never write any data back to Apple Health.
  • Activity and progress data generated in the App: your training history, XP, level, achievements, challenges, streaks, badges, and garage progress.

INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

JENNA does not collect:

  • Location data or GPS tracks
  • Heart rate data
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers

We do not use any advertising networks or third-party analytics trackers, and we do not sell your personal information.

APPLE HEALTH (HEALTHKIT) DATA

With your explicit permission, JENNA reads cycling and running workout summaries from Apple Health solely to display, track, and gamify your fitness within the App. In accordance with Apple's requirements:

  • We access Apple Health data on a read-only basis and never write data back to it.
  • We do not use Apple Health data for advertising or marketing.
  • We do not sell or share Apple Health data with third parties, data brokers, or for any purpose other than providing core App functionality.
  • You can revoke Health access at any time in the iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health, or within the Health app.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To create, secure, and manage your account.
  • To log your workouts and display your stats, streaks, and personal records.
  • To power gamification features (XP, levels, badges, challenges, garage).
  • To generate AI coaching nudges and weekly reflections based on your in-app training data.
  • To send push notifications (such as streak reminders) if you have enabled them.
  • To back up and sync your data across your devices.

AI-GENERATED INSIGHTS

JENNA generates coaching nudges and weekly reflections from the training data already in your account. Where this involves processing by a third-party AI service provider, only the data needed to generate the insight is processed, it is not used to serve you ads, and it is not sold. We do not use your data to train third-party AI models for unrelated purposes.

HOW YOUR DATA IS STORED

Your data is stored locally on your device and synced to our cloud backend, provided by Supabase, which hosts data in a PostgreSQL database protected by row-level security so that each account can only access its own data. Data in transit is encrypted using industry-standard TLS.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • Supabase — provides authentication and cloud storage/sync of your profile, workout history, and progress. See Supabase's privacy practices at https://supabase.com/privacy.
  • Apple HealthKit — provides read-only access to your workout summaries on your device, governed by Apple's privacy policies.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your account and activity data for as long as your account remains active. When you delete your account, we delete your associated personal data from our active systems, except where retention is required by law.

YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

  • Access & correction: you can view and update your information within the App.
  • Account & data deletion: you can delete your account and associated data from within the App (Settings > Delete Account) or by emailing us at the address below.
  • Health permission: you can grant or revoke Apple Health access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Notifications: you can disable push notifications in iOS Settings or in the App.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). California residents may request access to or deletion of their personal information and will not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

JENNA is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

INTERNATIONAL USERS

JENNA is operated from the United States. If you access the App from outside the U.S., you understand that your information may be processed and stored in the United States.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice.
